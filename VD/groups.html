Group Programs

Groups can be scheduled during off-peak hours or before regular business hours and must be scheduled in advance.

Birthday Parties

$225 for up to 8 climbers, $18 per additional. 2.5 hrs climbing, games, instruction, and party room use. Rentals included. 1 belayer per 4 kids. $50 non-refundable deposit.

Group Rate

$250 flat rate for up to 12 people, including harness & shoe rentals. $18 per additional person. We teach 1 adult belayer for every 3–4 climbers at no extra charge.

Group Lesson Rates

  • 1–4 people: $40 per person
  • 5–11 people: $30 per person
  • 12+ people: $25 per person

Includes belay instruction, climbing knots, rentals, and 3 hours at Vertical Dreams. Best for adults or teens 13+.

Corporate Programs & Team Building

Groups of 10+: $30/person, under 10: $300 minimum. Rock climbing builds communication, trust, and teamwork. Activities can be tailored for your group’s needs and experience level.

Overnights / Lock-ins (Nashua Only)

$600 for up to 15, $40 per additional person. Exclusive use, equipment, and supervision. Arrive 7–8 PM, climb, eat, sleep, leave by 9 AM. $100 deposit required.

Merit Badge Program

For Scouts 13+, 3-hour session with certified councilors. Covers climbing merit badge requirements. $260 for up to 4 scouts, $65 per additional. Appointment required.

Join Our Adult Climbing Team

$155 per member, $295 per non member, 10 week session. This Program is suitable for any adult looking to improve their climbing, and have some fun. Some prior experience is recommended. Make new friends and have fun!
