Outdoor Classes & Trips

Vertical Dreams is offering outdoor classes and trips to local climbing areas. NO EXPERIENCE NECESSARY. All technical equipment is provided with all our classes.

Possible climbing locations include: Pawtuckaway State Park, Rumney, NH, and Franconia Notch, NH.

For more information or to schedule a lesson, call Vertical Dreams or click the contact box to send us an email.

1-Day Rock Climbing Class

Beginner – Intermediate

Climbing with a Vertical Dreams guide. In this course, you’ll go climbing with one of our experienced guides and have the opportunity to try various climbs within your ability. All equipment is included. Choose from Pawtuckaway, Rumney, or Franconia Notch climbing areas.

  • 1 Person Private: $300
  • 2 people: $150 each
  • 3-6 people: $125 each
  • 6+ people: $100 each

Top Roping Instruction

Intermediate

In this course you’ll learn how to climb on your own. You will learn how to set up top rope anchors in various situations, as well as different rappelling techniques. Students should have taken our one or two-day classes or have some climbing experience.

  • 1 Person Private: $300
  • 2 people: $150 each
  • 3-6 people: $125 each
  • 6+ people: $100 each

Sport Climbing Instruction

Intermediate – Expert

This 1-day class is for experienced, intermediate top rope climbers who want to learn to lead climb. Students should have excellent belay skills and should be climbing 5.9.

  • 1 Person Private: $300
  • 2 people: $150 each
  • 3-6 people: $125 each
  • 6+ people: $100 each
