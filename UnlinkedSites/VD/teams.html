Climbing Teams and Programs

All teams meet weekly and are a 10 week course. Sign up in Advance, but late additions are welcome.

Join Our Kids' Climbing Club

Current session: July 7th to September 8th

$183 per member, $295 per non member, 10 week session, Meets Tuesdays 4pm-6pm. Current session ongoing. This program is suitable for any kid (6-12) looking to learn how to climb, play games, make new friends and have some fun! Our dedicated staff members will be there to supervise and belay. This allows climbers access to all climbing surfaces including our converted 70 foot elevator shaft! No experience necessary.

Join Our Youth Climbing Team

Upcoming session: September 11th to November 14th

$183 per member, $295 per non member, 10 week session, Meets Fridays 6pm-8:30pm. No current session. This program is suitable for any kid (13-17) looking to improve their climbing through a mixture of structured training (at their pace) and fun activities with their friends. We will go through terms and techniques, teach training methods, and make sure all climbers are having fun! No experience necessary.

Join Our Adult Climbing Team

Current session: July 7th to September 8th

$155 per member, $295 per non member, 10 week session, Meets Tuesdays 6pm-8:30pm. Current session ongoing. This Program is suitable for any adult (18+) looking to improve their climbing through tailored training meant to match their experience level. Learn the basics up to projecting expert level routes. No experience necessary.
